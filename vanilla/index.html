Unmodified Minecraft, Minecraft players use the term Vanilla Minecraft to describe the original, unmodified version of the game. A Vanilla Minecraft server includes no plug-ins or other mods, allowing players to play "pure," classic Minecraft. Because Minecraft encourages players to modify the game, few truly vanilla servers exist. Even some servers that claim to be vanilla may utilize a few basic, non-intrusive mods, such as the ability to use teleportation commands.
